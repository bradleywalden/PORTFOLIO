Dear Reader

I'm Bradley Walden, a modernist designer with a reductive and minimalistic visual linguage, living and working in California.

As a full stack developer with a taste for new challenges, I have had the opportunity to work in industries ranging from restaurants to factories to wigs and (you didn't hear this from me...) international smuggling.

My visual representations are usually ties to a literary past that when the piece is finally ready to be viewed the original intent would be abstracted so far that it could be represented by a single color off in a corner....but having it there is to know where this all sprang from.

I have a passion for learning new methods to create and build things so my work ranges from a single outlined vector to other worlds courtesy of Maxon and Autodesk. Enjoy the exploration. Don't get lost.
